## Features

- Arabic is the default experience with a clear language switch for English at any time.
- The entire interface is designed for right-to-left reading first, while fully supporting left-to-right layouts.
- Guests can explore public discussions, groups, services, events, and knowledge resources without signing in.
- Members can build a professional profile with role, industry, company, skills, certifications, experience, and reputation.
- Professionals can post questions, share insights, comment in threads, react to useful content, and follow relevant people.
- Topic-based communities let members join public or private groups around industries, business functions, and specialties.
- Direct messaging supports private chats and professional group conversations.
- Experts and consultants can publish service listings, receive requests, and build credibility through reviews.
- Organizations can create company pages, showcase expertise, discover consultants, and participate in communities.
- A dedicated governance and compliance hub gives professionals a structured place for regulatory updates, policy resources, and expert discussions.
- The knowledge hub organizes guides, templates, case studies, and reports in a searchable library.
- Events and webinars help experts host workshops, training, and professional sessions.
- A reputation system rewards helpful contributions, trusted service delivery, and consistent community engagement.
- Premium communities, subscriptions, and service transaction fees are designed into the platform from the start.
- Admin tools manage users, content quality, communities, marketplace approvals, and platform health.

## Design

- The product feels modern, credible, and business-first rather than casual or entertainment-driven.
- Arabic is treated as the native design language with strong hierarchy, elegant spacing, and polished typography.
- The visual style is clean and editorial, combining executive trust with community energy.
- Navigation feels structured and calm, with clear sections for community, messages, marketplace, knowledge, events, and profile.
- Cards, lists, and detail pages are optimized for dense professional information without looking crowded.
- Reputation, trust markers, expert badges, and company identity are visible but refined.
- Governance content feels serious and reliable, but remains integrated into the wider business ecosystem instead of overpowering it.
- Micro-interactions make reading, saving, reacting, and switching languages feel smooth and deliberate.
- App icon with a deep ink or emerald background and a refined geometric network mark that suggests business connection, trust, and knowledge flow.

## Pages / Screens

- Welcome screen that introduces the platform, highlights Arabic-first value, and offers sign in or guest browsing.
- Main home screen with a professional business feed, trending discussions, recommended communities, and important updates.
- Discussion detail screen with threaded replies, tags, reactions, moderation actions, and related resources.
- Communities hub showing featured groups, filters by industry and topic, and clear public or private access states.
- Group detail screen with discussion posts, announcements, shared resources, and member roles.
- Messaging inbox with conversation list, unread indicators, search, and quick access to direct or group chats.
- Chat screen for secure professional conversations, attachments, reactions, and read states.
- Marketplace home with featured services, categories, trusted experts, and request flows.
- Service detail screen with offer summary, pricing, delivery timeline, reviews, seller credibility, and request action.
- Governance hub with compliance categories, regulatory discussions, policy templates, and expert access.
- Knowledge hub with searchable resources, saved items, categories, and high-value business content.
- Events screen with upcoming webinars, workshops, registration flows, and attendance details.
- Profile screen for members, experts, and companies showing identity, expertise, reputation, activity, and services.
- Company page showing business overview, people, services, projects, and community presence.
- Admin dashboard for moderation, approvals, analytics, content review, and platform oversight.

## First Release Focus

- Launch with the strongest community foundation: feed, discussions, groups, messaging, marketplace, governance hub, knowledge hub, and events.
- Prioritize general business professionals and consultants as the first core audience.
- Design monetization around subscriptions, premium access, and marketplace commissions from the beginning.
- Keep the first release highly polished and structured, with room to expand deeper workflows after validation.

## Product Structure

- Public discovery layer for guests to explore trusted content before joining.
- Member collaboration layer for identity, networking, discussions, and messaging.
- Expert commerce layer for services, requests, reviews, and reputation.
- Governance layer for compliance knowledge, regulatory discussion, and specialist discovery.
- Organization layer for company presence, participation, and consultant hiring.
- Admin layer for platform trust, moderation, approvals, and insights.
